About Me

I am a 3rd-year Undergraduate student in Computer Science at San Francisco State Univeristy. I am currently working as a Researcher at Arizona State University under the guidance of Dr. Pooyan Fazli at the People and Robots Lab. My research interests include Human-Computer Interaction, Human-Robot Interaction, and Accessibility.
